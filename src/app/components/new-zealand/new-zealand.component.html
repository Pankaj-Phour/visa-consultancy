Study in New Zealand

Why Study in New Zealand ?

New Zealand, the land of the long white clouds, is an emerging top destination for a large number of international students. The picturesque landscape of the country will in itself scream the word “beautiful”. This beautiful country is growing to become one of the top desirable destinations among Indian students as well to study abroad.

New Zealand has a lot to offer with its top-class education system, internationally accredited qualifications, numerous options for higher education and research, and on top of all, a matchless quality of life. The world-class (yet affordable) Universities with student friendly and pleasant atmosphere could be the top most preference for your future. This is the reason why you can start a global career if you decide to study abroad in New Zealand. Read this blog to understand more about why you should choose New Zealand over other developed countries.

International Recognition

When you search for a University for your future the first thing that will come to your mind would be the recognition it has. But if you are planning on educating yourself in New Zealand you don’t have to worry because all eight Universities of New Zealand consistently rank in QS World Rankings and Times Higher Education Rankings. 

Therefore, a qualification from any of these institutions is accredited across the globe which will open the doors of prospect everywhere. Not only the highest Rankings but also the New Zealand Qualifications Authority (NZQA) assures that institutions are registered to enroll international students and comply with qualifications standards.

Standard Pedagogy

The education system of New Zealand is similar to that of the UK. They focus on research-based teaching which will allow the students to think critically and work their brain towards more creative ideas. The learning you would receive will be systematic and organized. The various groups, as well as independent tasks, will boost your knowledge on various job-related requirements. The student-teacher interaction will enhance the students to receive a comfortable atmosphere inside the learning environment.

Research and PhD

The abundant opportunities in research and innovation is a promise for a  quality future for you.   The highly experienced faculty, well-equipped laboratories, access to latest technology and opportunities will give a good academic exposure as well as the innovative spirit in which you will find the technology, research and development in various fields such as healthcare, physics, geology, engineering, astronomy, computer science, agriculture etc. which means a whole new opportunity to find the hidden talents in you. As a PhD student, along with the vast research opportunities, you would be enlisted to enjoy additional privileges, such as paying the same tuition fee as the locals and able to work full time during your studies, unlike the degree students.

Political Stability  

New Zealand is a country which can ensure your safety as well as a peaceful environment, which would be the top priority of your parents. According to the Global Peace Index of 2018, New Zealand ranks second. Thus, as an international student, you will have the same rights as your Kiwi counterpart. Thus we can assure you about the social tolerance and political stability of the Kiwi Democracy.

Standard of Living 

Life in New Zealand will provide every student with a chance to grow with its diverse cultural and natural beauty, all at once, because the multicultural environment that exists in New Zealand goes in perfect harmony with the country's outdoor lifestyle. 

Part-time Jobs

While studying in New Zealand you would be able to work to support your studies, you can work up to 20 hours every week during your semesters and full-time during breaks. And if you are pursuing research masters or a doctoral degree, you will be allowed to work full-time. 

Gorgeous Environment

New Zealand has a picturesque landscape which has a beautiful natural environment and it is particularly diverse. The name wanderlust suits the country as all kinds of natural landscapes ranging from snow-capped mountains and steaming volcanoes to rolling green hills, golden sandy beaches and lush rainforests. 

Less Population

New Zealand is a quite relaxed and stress-free country with a population of only 4.5 million. Your overall experience in New Zealand would be one of calmness and stress-free with one storey high houses having large gardens.

Pleasant Climate

The weather of New Zealand is very quiet throughout the year with a largely temperate climate. While the far north has subtropical weather during summer and inland alpine areas of the South Island can only be -10 C in winter, most of the country lies close to the coast, which means mild temperatures, moderate rainfall, and abundant sunshine.

Liberal Visa Application and Residency Process

New Zealand has liberal immigration laws unlike other countries which make it easy to obtain a student visa. Rather than giving a blunt rejection like most of the countries, New Zealand will give you a chance to speak to a visa expert in order to explain your case if they don’t accept your visa.

Furthermore, New Zealand proves to be the ideal destination for the people who are looking to discover and build a new life rather than just to live abroad for a few years. Because, after you graduate, you can work in the country for up to a year and can apply for permanent residency if you acquire a job which is related to the degree you studied.
